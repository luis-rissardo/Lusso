What is Lusso?

In the Italian language, Lusso means luxury. The word is often used to describe something that is extremely sophisticated, refined, and of high quality.
The association of the term Lusso with a luxury entity is related to the idea of exclusivity, opulence, and elegance.
When something is described as Lusso, it usually implies products, services, or experiences that are considered high-end and provide a luxurious lifestyle.

Therefore, we choose this term to represent what we stand for: High Grade Work for our customers!
However, the luxury is not the only aspect we care about, we also cherish safety, quality and appearence.
We value our clients and give them all the support necessary to maintain their products as intact as possible for a long period of time.

The company "Auto di Lusso" was founded 35 years ago as a car dealership and has since expanded to become a luxury vehicle dealership and customization shop.
With an unwavering focus on luxury and excellence, "Auto di Lusso" has built a solid reputation for offering an impeccable selection of high-quality cars and top-notch customization services.
With a dedicated team of automotive enthusiasts, the company continues to serve discerning customers who seek the perfect combination of elegance, exclusivity, and performance.

Our future objectives as a brand are:

1. Provide exceptional customer service by understanding and exceeding the unique needs and expectations of our clients.
2. Offer a curated selection of luxury vehicles that embody elegance, performance, and innovation.
3. Expand our range of customization services to provide clients with personalized and unique automotive experiences.
4. Foster long-term relationships with customers by delivering consistent quality, reliability, and transparency.
5. Stay at the forefront of industry trends and technological advancements to continually enhance our offerings.
